8th Annual Pflugerville Nativity Display

Every year our community comes together to put on one of the largest collections of Nativity displays. This event not only provides a wonderful family activity at the start of the Christmas season, but presents us with many wonderful opportunities for meaningful service as well.

This is a special time for us to come together as a Christian community and share in the magnificent meaning of the Christmas season. We hope you will join us and share in our joy as we celebrate the birth of our Savior Jesus Christ this holiday season.

To learn more about how you can participate, please visit the following pages to see how you can get involved!

  • For information related to the nativities to be displayed, please visit the Nativity page.
  • For information related to musical performances during the event, please visit the Music page.
  • For information related to refreshments, please visit the Cookies page.
  • For all other questions, please visit the Contact page and reach out to Terri Ogden.
  • Also, please visit and “like” our Facebook Page below to stay updated on the Pflugerville Nativity Display event each year! We hope to see you there!
